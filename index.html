About Automotive

Founded in 1985 by brothers John and James Wilson, Automotive has been serving the community with honest, reliable auto repair services for over 35 years.

What started as a small garage specializing in classic car restoration has grown into a full-service auto repair shop while maintaining our commitment to quality craftsmanship and customer satisfaction.

Certified Technicians

Our ASE-certified mechanics receive ongoing training on the latest automotive technologies.

Fair Pricing
