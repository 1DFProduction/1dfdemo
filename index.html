About The Toy Shop

Founded in 2005, The Toy Shop has been the trusted name for Japanese auto repair in our community. Our team of factory-trained technicians brings authentic Japanese craftsmanship to every repair.

We specialize exclusively in Japanese vehicles, allowing us to provide the most accurate diagnostics and repairs using genuine or OEM parts. Our attention to detail and commitment to quality sets us apart.
